Fairness-Constrained Resource Optimizer

Allocation Optimizer Input

Fairness Parameter (α - Alpha) 0.3

α = 0: Maximize Utility (allocates based purely on need).
α = 1: Maximize Equality (allocates equal shares regardless of population/need differences).

Max Utility (0.0) Equal Shares (1.0)

Mathematical Guarantee

Sharec = (1 - α) × (Needc / TotalNeed) + α × (1 / NumCamps)
